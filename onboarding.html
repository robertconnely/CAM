Onboarding & Day 1

Your first 90 days at Zelis are structured around three critical phases: Listen, Learn, and Lead.

Quick Summary

Your first 90 days at Zelis are structured around three phases: Listen (Days 1-30), Learn (Days 31-60), and Lead (Days 61-90). The Day 1 Readiness Checklist ensures you hit the ground running with pre-reads, key meetings scheduled, and systems access. The Listening Tour Playbook guides conversations with Jim Bridges, Serkan Kutan, Patrick O'Keefe, and key Product Directors. By Day 30, you'll have completed your diagnostic and identified your first quick wins.

The Three Phases

Phase 1: Listen (Days 1-30)

Your primary goal is to build relationships and understanding. Start with the Day 1 Readiness Checklist to ensure smooth onboarding. Schedule listening sessions with key leaders across the organization. The Listening Tour Playbook provides a structured approach to conversations, ensuring you cover product strategy, competitive positioning, and organizational dynamics.

Phase 2: Learn (Days 31-60)

Deepen your understanding of the PDLC Framework, capital allocation processes, and competitive landscape. Complete the Payment Integrity and OON Pricing study guides. Begin attending weekly product syncs and bi-weekly tech alignment meetings. Start mapping the product portfolio and understanding the M&A history.

Phase 3: Lead (Days 61-90)

By now you're ready to contribute independently. Identify and drive your first quick wins. Present your 90-day diagnostic to leadership. Propose initial initiatives aligned with the 2026-2028 product vision. Prepare for your first quarterly business review contribution.

Key Stakeholders

  • Jim Bridges - Product Leadership
  • Serkan Kutan - Technical Leadership
  • Patrick O'Keefe - Business Leadership
  • Product Directors across Payment Integrity and OON teams
  • Cross-functional partners in Engineering, Product, and Operations
