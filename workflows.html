Visual process flows for Payment Integrity, OON Pricing, and Revenue models.

Overview

Product workflows document the end-to-end processes that define how Zelis claims processing works. These diagrams illustrate claim flows, money flows, and processing logic across our core product lines. Understanding these workflows is essential for product strategy, capital allocation, and competitive positioning.

Payment Integrity Overview

Pre-payment editing intercepts claims before payment, preventing overpayments from being paid. Post-payment recovery identifies overpayments after the fact for recovery. The three branded diagrams above illustrate the core PI claim and money flows. Additional workflows for Clinical DRG Validation, FWA Detection, and Post-Payment Money Flow are in development with Zelis branding.

OON Pricing Workflows

Out-of-Network (OON) Pricing workflows cover claim repricing, Reference-Based Pricing (RBP), provider negotiation, and member experience. These processes help customers manage unexpected OON costs.

Zelis-branded diagrams in progress. OON Pricing FigJam boards (Repricing Process, Repricing Money Flow, RBP Process, RBP Money Flow, Provider Negotiation, Member Experience) are being updated to the Zelis brand color system. Figma embeds will be added once branding is complete.

OON Pricing Overview

Repricing takes OON charges and reprices them against in-network benchmarks or reference-based pricing rates. RBP sets payment benchmarks based on clinical factors, market rates, and negotiated agreements. Provider negotiation uses data insights to drive better rates. Member experience focuses on transparency and managing patient out-of-pocket costs.

Revenue Model Overview

Zelis operates on three revenue tiers: access fees (fixed annual contracts), per-claim processing fees (transaction-based), and savings share (percentage of customer savings). Understanding these flows is critical for financial projections and unit economics calculations.

Research & Reference

Key Takeaways

  • PI is Preventive: Pre-payment editing prevents overpayments; post-payment recovery identifies past errors
  • OON is Proactive: Repricing and RBP help customers manage unexpected high-cost care
  • Money Flow is Complex: Claims flow through multiple processing steps; savings accrue to payers and shared with Zelis
  • Integration is Key: Customers with both PI and OON solutions see greater total savings
  • Data Quality is Critical: Clean clinical and pricing data drives better results and higher savings

Using These Workflows

These workflow diagrams are tools for:

  • Sales Conversations: Explain our solution to prospective customers
  • Product Development: Identify opportunities for product enhancements
  • Customer Success: Train customers on how to use our platform
  • Strategic Planning: Visualize market opportunities and competitive differentiation
